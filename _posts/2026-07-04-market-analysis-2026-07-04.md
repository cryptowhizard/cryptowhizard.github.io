## Executive Summary
Bitcoin continues to command the market as total crypto capitalization sits at $2.25T and Bitcoin dominance remains elevated at 55.6%. Altcoin activity was mixed: a handful of small‑cap tokens delivered outsized moves (The Black Bull +96.87%), while some established projects retraced sharply (Solstice -21.46%). Sector flows show rotation into Meme and Layer‑2 assets even as AI tokens lag slightly.

## Market Overview
The global crypto market cap is $2.25 trillion, with Bitcoin dominance at 55.6%, indicating that more than half of market value resides in BTC. Bitcoin is trading at $62,461 and Ethereum at $1,756.96. These readings reflect a market that remains BTC‑centric: elevated dominance typically concentrates liquidity and reduces the amplitude of broad altcoin rallies, though pockets of speculative activity can still produce large individual gains.

Market breadth today was uneven. The concentration risk tied to Bitcoin means macro moves in BTC and macro financial conditions will likely continue to set the tone. However, sector rotations into specific narratives (meme, L2, RWA) are producing notable idiosyncratic returns for traders able to navigate higher volatility.

Actionable insight: For traders, consider scaling into confirmed strength and using tight risk controls (position sizing, trailing stops).
