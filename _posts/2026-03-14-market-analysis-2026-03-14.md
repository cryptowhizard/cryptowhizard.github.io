That pattern is consistent with a market where macro or structural drivers are steady but traders are chasing idiosyncratic momentum in individual projects.

- Momentum: The market shows mixed momentum — concentrated strong momentum in certain small caps versus weakening momentum across many sector groups. That profile favors selective, event-driven trading rather than broad directional bets.

## Market Outlook — What to watch
- Dominance flow: Watch Bitcoin dominance for signs of renewed altcoin rotation. A falling dominance could open a broader altcoin rebound; sustained high dominance tends to compress gains outside BTC.
- Liquidity and listings: New exchange listings, token unlock schedules, or major announcements can rapidly change momentum for small/mid-cap tokens — monitor project calendars and reputable news sources.
- On-chain and derivatives indicators: Funding rates, open interest, and exchange flows for BTC/ETH can flag emerging leverage risks that precede sharp moves.
- Sector catalysts: For DeFi, L1/L2, and AI sectors, protocol upgrades, regulatory headlines, and partnership announcements will be primary drivers. Track these carefully.
