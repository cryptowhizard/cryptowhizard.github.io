The crypto market moved higher on May 9, 2026, with total capitalization at $2.76T and Bitcoin dominance at 58.2%. Bitcoin traded at $80,260 while Ethereum was at $2,312.48; several mid-cap and thematic tokens—most notably Constellation (DAG)—posted outsized gains, while a small cohort of smaller caps pulled back.

The aggregate market cap of $2.76 trillion and Bitcoin dominance of 58.2% paint a picture of a market that remains BTC-centric but still allows room for selective altcoin rallies. Bitcoin’s price of $80,260 and Ethereum’s $2,312.48 reflect ongoing concentration in the largest assets even as rotative flows into thematic sectors (AI, privacy, gaming) are evident. Overall intraday breadth favored gainers, but volatility remains elevated in smaller-cap names.
