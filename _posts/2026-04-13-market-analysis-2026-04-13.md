## Executive Summary
The crypto market showed a mixed session with a total market cap of $2.49T and Bitcoin maintaining dominant market share at 56.9%. RaveDAO (RAVE) led the rallies with a dramatic +255.22% surge to $9.24, while SkyAI (SKYAI) was the largest decliner, falling 12.75% to $0.11812. Sector breadth was uneven: Privacy was the only positive sector, while Gaming, DeFi and AI showed weakness.

## Market Overview
Total market capitalization sits at $2.49 trillion, signaling that the macro-sized crypto market remains sizable and comparatively stable at current levels. Bitcoin dominance is 56.9%, indicating that more than half of the market value remains concentrated in Bitcoin. Bitcoin is trading at $70,865 and Ethereum at $2,186.97 — both remain the primary anchors for price action and risk sentiment across altcoins.

The market profile today reflects a classic leadership dynamic: large-cap cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin and Ethereum) continue to steer risk appetite while single-asset narratives produce outsized moves on idiosyncratic news or low float dynamics. Overall liquidity appears sufficient for large moves in small-cap tokens while broader sectors show marginal declines.

- SkyAI (SKYAI): -12.75% at $0.11812 — notable today as the largest loser. A double-digit drop like this can reflect profit-taking after prior gains, negative project-specific news, or a broader risk-off rotation within the AI/ML crypto niche.
- PIEVERSE, ALEO, ARC, ARIA: declines between ~8.5%–12% indicate pressure across lower-cap projects.

Sector-level weakness in AI (-0.80%), DeFi (-1.13%), L1 (-0.78%) and Gaming (-1.50%) suggests a rotation away from riskier, narrative-driven verticals into either safety (Bitcoin) or selective alts (Privacy). Traders should watch for follow-through: multiple days of sector outperformance or underperformance confirm rotation; single-day moves can quickly reverse.

Risk management note: When positioning in underperforming assets, confirm whether selling is driven by fundamentals (security issues, tokenomics changes) or transient liquidity needs. Avoid averaging down into projects lacking clear recovery catalysts.

Overall trend assessment: The market exhibits mixed momentum — strong, concentrated upside in select small caps, with broader sectors showing mild weakness.
