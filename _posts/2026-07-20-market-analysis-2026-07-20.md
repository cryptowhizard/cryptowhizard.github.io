- Ethereum: Price is also in an uptrend but shows less relative strength versus Bitcoin on a dominance-adjusted basis. ETH’s trend remains intact while episodes of consolidation are likely as traders digest gains.
- Altcoins: The altcoin landscape is bifurcated. Small caps demonstrate high volatility and episodic momentum spikes (e.g., BANK). Trend strength in these names is unstable—sustained moves typically require follow-through volume or concrete on-chain/adoption signals.

Trading guidance: Favor momentum-confirmed setups with clear volume support. Manage exposure size in small-cap trades, maintain disciplined stops, and avoid over-leveraging in low-liquidity tokens. Use scaling strategies for re-entry and consider time-based exits if catalysts do not materialize.
