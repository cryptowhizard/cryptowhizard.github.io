The crypto market showed mixed internals on December 13, 2025: total market cap stands at $3.17T with Bitcoin dominance at 57.0%, and BTC trading at $90,519 while ETH trades at $3,123.89. This divergence suggests concentration of returns among smaller, more volatile tokens even as the core market remains comparatively stable.
