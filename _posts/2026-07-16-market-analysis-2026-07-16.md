## Market Overview
Total market cap: $2.29T. Bitcoin dominance: 56.2%. Bitcoin is reported at $64,120 and Ethereum at $1,882.69. Those readings indicate a market still centralized around BTC — over half of the ecosystem’s market value remains concentrated in Bitcoin. That structural concentration often translates into broader market sensitivity to BTC price action: when BTC consolidates or drifts, smaller caps and altcoin sectors typically show exaggerated volatility as traders rotate risk.

Net market behavior on the session was mixed rather than uniformly bullish or bearish. With notable single-asset rallies and declines, liquidity and idiosyncratic flows appear to be driving price dispersion across tokens.

Actionable insight: For traders, these names offer high short-term volatility opportunities but carry heightened execution and liquidity risk. Use tight risk controls and limit exposure sizes. For longer-term investors, validate fundamental news and on-chain metrics before committing capital.
