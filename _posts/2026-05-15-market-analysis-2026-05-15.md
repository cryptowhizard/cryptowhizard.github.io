Total Market Cap: $2.73T • Bitcoin Dominance: 58.1%

For directional traders, favor setups with clear momentum confirmation and manageable position sizing. - Liquidity and breadth: A recovery that broadens beyond top caps into sectors (DeFi, L1/L2) would be a constructive next step for a sustained market advance; persistent narrow breadth would suggest a consolidation or rotation phase.
