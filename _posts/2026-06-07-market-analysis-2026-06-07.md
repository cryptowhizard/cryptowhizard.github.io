## Market Overview
Total crypto market cap sits at $2.16 trillion, reflecting a day in which market leadership consolidated toward Bitcoin — dominance is 56.2%. That rising dominance, combined with continued weakness across many altcoin sectors, suggests capital rotated out of smaller and mid-cap tokens back into larger, perceived safer assets. BTC trading at $60,653 and ETH at $1,556.31 represent the reference anchor prices for the market today.

Volatility remains present: several single-name rallies posted double- and triple-digit intraday percentage gains, while other projects corrected sharply. Overall liquidity conditions appear to favor large-cap accumulation and selective risk-on activity rather than broad-based altcoin rallies.
