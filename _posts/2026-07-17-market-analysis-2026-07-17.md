## Market Outlook — What to Watch
- Layer-2 adoption signals: Continued outperformance of L2s should be validated by bridge inflows, active addresses, and developer activity. If metrics confirm, expect further rotation into L2 ecosystems.
- BTC dominance and macro headlines: A rising dominance could limit broad altcoin rallies; macro events (rates, equities) remain key catalysts.
- Liquidity concentration in small caps: Monitor exchanges and order-book depth; sudden declines like the CASHCAT move can cascade in low-liquidity tokens.
- Regulatory and custodial developments: Any new guidance or enforcement action can rapidly shift risk appetite across DeFi and RWA sectors.

For traders: focus on momentum-confirmed entries, keep position sizes aligned with liquidity, and use tight risk controls. For investors: prioritize projects with durable fundamentals and transparent governance; consider staged buy strategies rather than one-off large allocations in volatile names.
