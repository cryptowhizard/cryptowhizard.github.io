The crypto market moved higher on July 3 with a total market capitalization of $2.20T and Bitcoin maintaining a majority share at 55.8% dominance. Risk-on flows favored higher-beta names: MemeCore surged as the top gainer (+78.34%), while Velvet led decliners with a heavy loss (-63.61%). Sector rotation into DeFi, Layer 1s and Privacy themes stands out.

- Total Market Cap: $2.20T

- ETH Price: $1,646.28

The market is in a broadly constructive phase, with aggregate capitalization holding above multi-trillion-dollar levels. Bitcoin’s dominance at 55.8% indicates that BTC remains the primary liquidity attractor, but meaningful outperformance in alt sectors shows investors are willing to allocate risk capital into smaller-cap and thematic plays. Volatility is elevated, evidenced by double- and triple-digit percentage swings across individual tokens.
