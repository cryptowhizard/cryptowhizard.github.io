## Executive Summary
The crypto market advanced today as total market capitalization rose to $3.22T while Bitcoin maintained a dominant position at 57.0% market share. Risk appetite favored privacy and select altcoins, with Zcash surging as the day's top gainer, while legacy chain LUNC and several small-cap tokens suffered sharp declines.

## Market Overview
The aggregate market cap stands at $3.22 trillion, reflecting a broad but selective risk-on tone across digital assets. Bitcoin dominance remains elevated at 57.0%, indicating that more than half of market value is concentrated in BTC even as select altcoins outperformed on a percentage basis. Bitcoin is trading at $92,004 and Ethereum at $3,158.33 — both key reference points for portfolio risk allocation and hedging decisions.

Elevated dominance alongside selective altcoin strength suggests institutional or large-cap accumulation in BTC coexisting with episodic speculative flows into specific sectors and smaller-cap names.
