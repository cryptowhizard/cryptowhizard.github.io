## Executive Summary
The crypto market advanced notably on November 30, 2025, with total capitalization at $3.18T and bitcoin dominance steady at 57.2%. Bitcoin traded at $91,276 and Ether at $3,008.25 while AI-led tokens drove outsized gains; the AI sector surged +52.91% overall. Volatility was pronounced — some small- and mid-cap names posted triple-digit swings while others posted sharp pullbacks.

## Market Overview
Total market cap: $3.18 trillion. Bitcoin dominance: 57.2%, with BTC trading at $91,276 and ETH at $3,008.25. The market profile today skewed toward risk-on sentiment, concentrated in thematic pockets rather than broad-based strength. While major caps remained firm, much of the upward momentum came from high-beta sectors and individual token performances, driving headline numbers but increasing concentration risk.

Liquidity conditions appear supportive for larger caps: BTC and ETH held above key psychological levels (see prices above), anchoring market confidence. However, the distribution of gains — a few tokens driving large percentage moves — suggests speculative flows and rotation rather than uniform directional commitment across the ecosystem.

## Technical Analysis (Qualitative)
Momentum: Market momentum is positive overall, led by sectoral leadership in AI. BTC and ETH are showing constructive price action at present price levels mentioned above, which supports broader risk appetite.

Trend strength: The trend for large caps appears intact, but breadth is weak — the market advance is narrow. This divergence (strong leaders, many laggards) typically precedes short-term bouts of volatility as capital rotates.

Risk levels: Elevated for small- and mid-cap tokens due to heightened intraday swings (e.g., PIPPIN +63%, SAHARA -43%). Volatility favors active traders but raises tail-risk for passive holders. Position sizing, use of stop-losses, and avoiding overconcentration in single names are prudent.

Trading insights:
- Favor liquidity: prioritize tokens with deep on-chain activity and tight spreads.
- Use scaled entries: build positions in tranches to manage downside.
- Protect gains: for profitable short-term trades, consider trailing stops or partial profit-taking given rapid swings.

## Market Outlook
Near-term, watch for two dynamics:
1. AI sector leadership sustainability — whether gains extend across multiple snapshots or narrow to a handful of tokens. Continued inflows into AI-themed tokens would maintain a risk-on tone; a reversal could trigger a broader retracement given the current narrow breadth.
2. BTC/ETH stability — with BTC at $91,276 and ETH at $3,008.25, their price behavior will determine broader market conviction. A stable or strengthening BTC/ETH backdrop would support risk-on flows; sharp weakness could cascade into small-cap corrections.

Key indicators to monitor:
- Sector rotation flows (from on-chain transfers and exchange flows).
- Volume and liquidity trends in top movers.
- News and fundamental developments for the largest single-day movers (both positive catalysts and adverse announcements).
