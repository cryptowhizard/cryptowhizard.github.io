Bitcoin traded at $60,256 and Ether at $1,579.45 while Velvet (VELVET) led the day with a dramatic +153.06% surge. Total market cap sits at $2.17 trillion, reflecting a market in constructive posture but not in a runaway risk-on state. Bitcoin dominance is a relatively high 55.8%, indicating that BTC continues to capture the majority of inflows and attention versus altcoins. BTC is trading at $60,256 and ETH at $1,579.45 — these levels anchor market sentiment and are likely constraining leverage and risk appetite across the broader market.

The market environment today shows controlled bullishness: more sectors are positive than negative, but leadership is mixed between BTC/ETH and select altcoin themes. With BTC dominance above the mid-50s, rotation into smaller-cap names is present but selective.
