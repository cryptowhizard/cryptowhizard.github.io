## Executive Summary
The crypto market closed with a total market cap of $3.63T while Bitcoin dominance sits at 57.7%, signaling concentrated strength in the largest assets even as sector rotation and idiosyncratic moves drove volatility. MetaDAO led gainers with a strong one-day jump of +32.18% while DeAgentAI plunged -45.99%, highlighting continued dispersion between winners and losers.

## Market Overview
Total market capitalization: $3.63T. Bitcoin dominance: 57.7%. Bitcoin and Ethereum remain the market anchors at $105,160 and $3,559.66 respectively, accounting for a large share of market liquidity and headline flows. The elevated dominance figure indicates capital remains concentrated in the largest, most liquid assets rather than broadly diffusing into smaller caps. That concentration can mute broad-market rallies while amplifying declines in thinner tokens when sentiment shifts.

Market breadth is mixed: a handful of mid- and small-cap tokens posted double-digit gains, but multiple sectors and notable projects recorded steep losses, resulting in a choppy, range-driven backdrop rather than a clear, risk-on breakout across the board.
