The combination of a large-cap anchor and selective altcoin volatility has produced a market where headline cap is stable while dispersion across coins increases.

## Market Outlook
Near term, expect continued concentration of capital around Bitcoin and a handful of high-liquidity altcoins, with episodic flash rallies and reversions in smaller projects. Key catalysts to monitor:
- Exchange listings or delistings for illiquid tokens that can trigger outsized moves.
- Protocol announcements, on-chain upgrade schedules, and major partnerships for L1/L2 and DeFi tokens.
- Macro liquidity signals (central bank commentary, US CPI/PPI, rate decisions) that will influence risk appetite and correlate with Bitcoin flows.
- Funding rates and leverage metrics across derivatives markets to anticipate forced liquidations.

Traders should watch for confirmation of sustained volume when a small-cap breaks out; absent that, treat moves as potential squeezes.
