- Total market cap: $2.44T — the market remains large-cap dominated.

## Market Outlook — What to Watch
- Bitcoin dominance: Watch whether BTC dominance expands further above current levels (56.3%). Continued expansion would typically indicate risk-off rotation into BTC and away from altcoins.
- Macro inputs: Broader macro headlines (rate decisions, economic prints) can shift risk appetite quickly; monitor for events that could catalyze marketwide moves.
- On-chain and flow indicators: For validation of altcoin moves, look for concurrent increases in active addresses, token transfers, and exchange inflows/outflows.
- Sector cross-confirmation: If L2 and Privacy sectors continue to show relative strength while L1/DeFi lag, capital may be rotating into utility and infrastructure rather than purely speculative returns.
- News and listings: Sharp moves in small caps are often tied to exchange listings or project-specific announcements — confirm before deploying capital.
