## Market Overview
The overall market sits at a sizable $2.51 trillion, with Bitcoin commanding 57.1% of capital allocation — a level that generally signals consolidation of risk toward BTC and away from altcoins. BTC trading at $71,322 and ETH at $2,192.28 provide the baseline for market sentiment: when BTC strength persists, risk-on flows into altcoins can be selective and contingent on momentum, liquidity, and sector rotation.

Net market behavior today was mixed: while headline gains from a few speculative large-percent movers drew attention, most tracked sectors posted modest declines, pointing to cautious positioning and profit-taking across a broad cross-section of tokens.
