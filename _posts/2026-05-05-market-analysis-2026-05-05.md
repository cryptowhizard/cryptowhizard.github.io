- BTC dominance: At 58.7%, any sustained shift higher would typically signal a contraction in altcoin leadership; a drop could accelerate altcoin rallies.
- Sector leadership rotation: Continue monitoring L1/L2 and RWA flows for signs of durable capital allocation vs. short-term rotation.
- On-chain indicators: Exchange inflows/outflows, large-wallet transfers, and staking/borrowing activity will be key to determining whether current gains are sustainable.

Actionable checklist: monitor BTC dominance and exchange flow metrics daily, scale positions into larger-sized trades, and favor liquidity and transparent tokenomics when allocating capital.
