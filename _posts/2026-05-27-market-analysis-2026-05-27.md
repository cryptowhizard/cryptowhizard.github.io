## Executive Summary
The crypto market showed a constructive risk-on tone as total capitalization rose to $2.65T while Bitcoin dominance remained elevated at 58.1%. Bitcoin traded at $76,951 and Ether at $2,115.41, with AI-sector tokens leading gains and select small- and mid-cap names showing volatile intraday moves.

## Market Overview
The aggregate crypto market cap sits at $2.65 trillion, reflecting continued investor appetite for digital assets at scale. Bitcoin dominance at 58.1% indicates BTC still commands the majority share of market value, reinforcing its role as the primary liquidity anchor and risk benchmark. With BTC at $76,951 and ETH at $2,115.41, large-cap correlation remains a key driver for broader market direction; shifts in Bitcoin momentum are likely to cascade across altcoins, while sector-specific flows are producing notable dispersion beneath the surface.
