The crypto market closed December 26 with a total market capitalization of $3.07T and Bitcoin dominance at 57.7%, as BTC traded at $88,740 and ETH at $2,967.44. Market action was mixed but tilted modestly risk-on: selective altcoins posted strong gains while a handful of projects suffered sharp drawdowns.

Total crypto market cap: **$3.07T**. Bitcoin dominance at **57.7%** underscores continued leadership by BTC in market flows, with traders rotating capital between large-cap crypto and selective altcoins. Bitcoin's price at **$88,740** remains the central reference for sentiment and liquidity; Ethereum traded at **$2,967.44**, continuing to trade in close correlation to broader risk appetite. The market environment today featured concentrated winners rather than broad-based upside, indicating constructive but cautious risk-taking.
