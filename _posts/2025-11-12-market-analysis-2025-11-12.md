The crypto market posted a mixed session with a total market cap of **$3.61T** and **Bitcoin dominance at 57.9%**. Bitcoin traded at **$104,697** and Ethereum at **$3,496.21** while small- and mid-cap names led gains (Meteora +27.92%), even as several altcoins and sector pockets showed weakness.

Today's action shows selective risk-on behavior: large-cap dominance continues, but meaningful move in small-caps suggests short-term appetite for idiosyncratic upside.

## Market Outlook
Near term, expect continued selective risk-taking. Key drivers to monitor:
- Bitcoin and Ethereum price stability and volatility — they set the backdrop for risk appetite.
- Volume confirmation behind top gainers — sustained upside requires follow-through volume and on-chain adoption signals.
- Macro/regulatory headlines — any significant news can quickly re-order risk preferences and widen sector dispersion.
- Derivatives metrics — funding rates and open interest can accelerate moves, particularly in small-cap names.

For investors: consider a barbell approach — core exposure to BTC/ETH (given dominance and market cap) with a smaller, actively managed allocation to high-conviction altcoin opportunities. For traders: favor momentum strategies with clear exit rules and prioritize liquidity.
