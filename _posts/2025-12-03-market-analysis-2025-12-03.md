## Executive Summary
The crypto market posted strong gains as total market capitalization rose to $3.24 trillion and Bitcoin dominance held elevated at 57.4%. AI-related tokens led a broad-based rally — the AI sector jumped 69.32% — while select mid- and large-cap names lagged, with FOLKS down 18.75%.

## Market Overview
The aggregate crypto market cap sits at $3.24T, with Bitcoin maintaining a majority share at 57.4% dominance. Bitcoin is trading at $93,191 and Ethereum at $3,062.38. The market environment is characterized by strong sector rotation into AI and meme assets, elevated intraday volatility across small-cap tokens, and continued concentration of value in Bitcoin (over half the market by dominance). Overall breadth skewed positive today, with more sectors showing double-digit gains at the sub-sector level and only isolated deep losses among individual tokens.
