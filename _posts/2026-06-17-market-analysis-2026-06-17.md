## Executive Summary
The crypto market finished mixed on June 17, with total capitalization at **$2.33T** and Bitcoin maintaining a dominant share (**56.4%**). Bitcoin traded at **$65669** and Ethereum at **$1780.5** as leadership remained concentrated in large caps while smaller tokens produced outsized single-day moves — Block Street (BSB) led gainers with **+42.07%** while Bitway (BTW) tumbled **-32.47%**.

## Market Overview
The aggregate market cap of **$2.33T** and Bitcoin dominance of **56.4%** indicate a market environment still skewed toward BTC-led moves. Bitcoin at **$65669** and Ethereum at **$1780.5** anchor liquidity and risk appetite: BTC’s dominance above 50% suggests that capital flows are favoring the largest, more liquid assets rather than a broad-based altcoin rally. Overall market breadth was tepid: while a handful of small- and mid-cap tokens produced strong single-day returns, sector-level averages were largely negative.
