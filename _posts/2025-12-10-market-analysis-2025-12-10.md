## Market Overview
The aggregated market cap of $3.26 trillion reflects continued institutional and retail liquidity in cryptocurrencies. Bitcoin dominance at 56.8% indicates that more than half of the market's value remains concentrated in BTC, but the day’s sector performance shows meaningful rotation into altcoins. Volume patterns and percentage moves suggest risk-on behavior: several sectors recorded multi-percent gains, implying buying interest beyond just BTC and ETH. The market breadth—positive across most tracked sectors—signals a healthy advance rather than a narrow rally.
