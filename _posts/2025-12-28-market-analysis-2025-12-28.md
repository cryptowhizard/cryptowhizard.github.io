## Executive Summary
Market risk appetite showed up on December 28, 2025, with the total crypto market capitalization at $3.06T and broad upside across multiple sectors. Bitcoin remains central to market structure at a dominance of 57.3% while several mid- and small-cap tokens led daily percentage gains. Conversely, a handful of liquidity-sensitive altcoins saw notable declines.

## Market Overview
The crypto market totaled $3.06 trillion today, with Bitcoin dominance at 57.3%, signaling a market still concentrated in large caps even as altcoins staged selective rallies. Bitcoin traded at $87,837 and Ethereum at $2,939.61; these two coins continue to account for the majority of market capitalization and set risk-on / risk-off tone for the rest of the market. Net sector returns were broadly positive, suggesting risk-on flows rather than a defensive posture.

Such a move is typical for low-price, low-market-cap tokens where relatively modest buying can produce large percentage gains. Traders should check volume and on-chain activity for confirmation before extrapolating momentum.

- **Flow (FLOW)**: -19.35% at $0.119167 — weakness in a previously resilient ecosystem token that could reflect profit-taking or broader altcoin rotation away from specific chains.

Traders and investors should verify on-chain transfer patterns, exchange inflows, and project announcements before assuming a change in fundamental outlook.
