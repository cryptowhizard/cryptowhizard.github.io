## Technical Analysis (Qualitative)
- Bitcoin (BTC at $68,957) and Ethereum (ETH at $2,014.22) are anchoring market sentiment. With BTC dominance at 56.9%, the market remains correlated to Bitcoin’s moves; a decisive directional move in BTC will likely compress or expand altcoin volatility.
- Momentum: Short-term momentum is risk-on for small caps (as evidenced by multiple double-digit percentage winners), but breadth is limited—gains are not yet broad-based across medium-to-large caps.
- Trend strength: Bitcoin’s large market share suggests that the higher-timeframe trend for the market is best gauged on BTC price action and on-chain metrics. For altcoins, trend strength is heterogeneous—many winners display strong short-term momentum but elevated vulnerability to reversals.
- Risk levels: Elevated for small-cap altcoins due to low liquidity and concentration risk. Systemic risk appears moderate given the small magnitude of sector gains and the lack of a sweeping market-wide rally.

Practical trading rules for current conditions:
- Use tighter position sizing and stop-losses on low-cap alts.
- Confirm breakouts with volume and/or on-chain metrics before adding exposure.
- Monitor BTC dominance for signs of alt rotation (a falling dominance typically precedes broader altcoin strength).
